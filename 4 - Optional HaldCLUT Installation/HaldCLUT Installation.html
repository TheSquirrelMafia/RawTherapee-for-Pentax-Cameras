The HaldCLUT folder has all the film simulation settings.

You can copy the folder anywhere you want to in your PC.

When you open up RawTherapee, go to:

Preferences > Image Processing

Scroll down to HaldCLUT directory & set it to wherever you placed the HaldCLUT folder.

Restart RawTherapee.

Once you open a RAW file, you can access the Film Simlations in the Color tab. Just scroll down to Film Simulation, turn it on, & select the film simulation you want to use.
